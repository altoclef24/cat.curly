Tennessee Rex

The Tennessee Rex is a gentle, social breed that loves to spend time with their family. They like to seek out thier humans around the house, curl up on the couch, and sleep on the bed at night. They're usually quiet cats, but have playful personalities. They are medium to large cats, with curly fur caused by a naturally occurring mutation.
